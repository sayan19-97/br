Welcome To

BrainRush 2k17

BrainRush is an intra-college Quiz competition hosted for the 3rd time at MCKVIE under the CSI Student Chapter Activities. BrainRush promises to test the inquizzitiveness of the contenstants with some brainstorming quizzing sessions. After the success of BrainRush 2k15 and 2k16, this year promises to be more enlighting, illuminating and fun. The event is going to take place on 15th November, at MCKVIE campus. We would also like to thank our sponsors in advance for sponsoring this event. So hope you all turn up for MCKVIE's mega quiz show. Till then "Keep Calm and Happy Quizzing"
